Planning your consultation

A little preparation helps make your session more useful.

Share accurate birth details

For most chart-based readings, provide your date of birth, exact or best-known birth time, and birthplace. Mention if the birth time is approximate.

Identify your most important questions

You may have several concerns, but noting the decisions or patterns that matter most helps the consultation remain focused and practical.

Choose in-person or online

Appointments can be arranged at the Vadodara or Bharuch office, or online for clients throughout India and worldwide.

Expect a private conversation

Your information and the subjects discussed are treated respectfully and confidentially. Guidance is intended to support your decisions without judgment or fear.
