Choosing your reading

Different questions may require different kinds of analysis.

Begin with the situation, not the service name

You do not need to identify the exact consultation yourself. Explain the decision, concern, or transition you are facing, and the team can guide you toward the most relevant reading.

Some questions benefit from a complete chart

When several areas of life are connected, a full Birth Chart Analysis or Vedic Astrology reading can reveal the larger pattern before focusing on one subject.

Focused readings go deeper into one area

Relationship, career, business, financial, numerology, medical-awareness, and Vastu consultations examine the factors most relevant to that specific concern.

Every consultation remains personal

Two clients asking the same question may receive very different guidance because their charts, timing, circumstances, strengths, and choices are different.
