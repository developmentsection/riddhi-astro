Policy.

This policy explains how Riddhi Astrology collects, uses, protects, and respects information shared through the website and during consultations.

Riddhi Astrology recognizes that birth details, personal questions, and consultation conversations can be sensitive. We are committed to handling the information entrusted to us carefully, privately, and only for legitimate purposes connected with your enquiry or requested service.

1. About this policy

This Privacy Policy applies when you visit this website, contact Riddhi Astrology by phone, WhatsApp, email, Instagram, or another communication channel, request an appointment, or participate in a consultation.

By voluntarily providing information, you acknowledge that it may be handled as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please avoid submitting personal information and contact us to discuss any concern before booking.

2. Information we may collect

The information collected depends on how you interact with the practice and may include:

  • Name, phone number, email address, city, and preferred contact method.
  • Birth date, exact or approximate birth time, birthplace, and related chart information.
  • Relationship, family, career, business, financial, wellbeing, or other questions voluntarily shared for a consultation.
  • Appointment preferences, payment confirmation, consultation history, and service-related correspondence.
  • Basic technical information generated when visiting the website, such as browser type, device type, and pages viewed, where such information is made available by hosting or analytics services.

3. How information is collected

Information may be collected directly from you through the website enquiry form, WhatsApp, telephone conversations, email, social media messages, appointment discussions, and during consultations. The website enquiry form prepares a WhatsApp message using the details you enter; it does not independently create a public account or profile.

We may also receive information from a family member or representative arranging a consultation on your behalf. That person is responsible for having appropriate permission to share your details.

4. How information is used

Personal information may be used to respond to enquiries, recommend an appropriate consultation, schedule and confirm appointments, prepare birth charts or related analysis, conduct the requested consultation, provide follow-up clarification, maintain administrative records, and improve the quality of services.

Information is not sold, rented, or traded for advertising. Marketing messages will only be sent where reasonably expected or permitted, and you may ask us to stop them at any time.

5. Sensitive and consultation information

Questions discussed during a consultation are treated as private. Please share only the information you are comfortable providing and that is relevant to the requested guidance.

Astrological consultations are not medical, mental-health, legal, or financial professional services. Avoid sending medical reports, identification documents, banking details, passwords, or other highly sensitive records unless specifically necessary and agreed in advance.

6. Storage, retention, and security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Information may be stored in appointment records, communication platforms, chart-preparation tools, or secure devices used to provide the service.

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for consultation preparation, client communication, administration, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and legitimate business records. No internet transmission or storage method can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Sharing and service providers

Information may be processed through trusted service providers required for communication, maps, website hosting, payment handling, or appointment administration. These may include WhatsApp, email providers, Instagram, Google Maps, hosting providers, and payment platforms selected during booking.

Information may also be disclosed where required by applicable law, a lawful authority, or when reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or prevent misuse. We do not authorize service providers to use consultation information for their own unrelated purposes.

8. External links and third-party platforms

The website contains links to third-party platforms. When you follow a link or communicate through another platform, its own privacy policy and security practices apply. Riddhi Astrology does not control those services and cannot be responsible for their independent handling of information.

9. Your choices and requests

Subject to applicable law and legitimate record-keeping requirements, you may ask to access, correct, update, or delete personal information held by Riddhi Astrology. You may also withdraw consent for future non-essential communication.

To protect privacy, we may request reasonable verification before acting on a request. Some information may need to be retained where required for legal, accounting, security, or dispute-resolution purposes.

10. Children's privacy

Services involving a minor should be requested by a parent or legal guardian. A parent or guardian is responsible for deciding what information to provide and for participating where appropriate.

11. Policy updates and contact

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in services, technology, or legal requirements. The current version will remain available on this page.
